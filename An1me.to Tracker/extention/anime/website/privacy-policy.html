1. Introduction

Welcome to An1me.to Tracker ("we," "our," or "the Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Our Commitment: We are committed to protecting your privacy. We only collect the minimum information necessary to provide our services, and we never sell your personal data.

By using the An1me.to Tracker extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Email Address: Your Google account email address for identification purposes
  • Profile Name: Your Google account display name
  • Profile Picture URL: The URL to your Google profile picture

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

The extension automatically collects the following data while you use an1me.to:

  • Anime Watch History: The titles of anime you watch on an1me.to
  • Episode Progress: Which episodes you have watched and your progress within each episode
  • Watch Time: Duration of videos watched (used to calculate statistics)
  • Timestamps: When you watched specific episodes
  • Video Progress: Current playback position for resume functionality

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your browsing history outside of an1me.to
  • Personal files or documents
  • Passwords or financial information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Any content from other websites

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Identification: Your email and profile information are used to create and identify your account for cloud synchronization
  • Progress Tracking: Anime and episode data are used to track your watching progress
  • Cloud Sync: Your data is synced across browsers and devices so you can continue watching from where you left off
  • Resume Playback: Video progress is saved to allow you to resume watching
  • Statistics: Watch time data is used to show you personal statistics about your viewing habits
  • Filler Detection: Episode data is used to identify and display filler episodes

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your anime watching data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API (chrome.storage.local). This data remains on your device unless you choose to sync it.

4.2 Cloud Storage

When you sign in with Google, your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore. Firebase provides:

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL)
  • Secure data storage with access controls
  • Data isolation between users

4.3 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Firebase authentication tokens are used for secure access
  • User data is isolated and only accessible by the authenticated user
  • No sensitive credentials are stored in the extension

5. Third-Party Services

Our extension uses the following third-party services:

5.1 Google Sign-In (OAuth 2.0)

We use Google Sign-In for authentication. When you sign in:

  • Google handles the authentication process
  • We only receive the scopes we request: email and profile information
  • Your Google password is never shared with us

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.2 Firebase (Google Cloud)

We use Firebase Firestore for cloud data storage and Firebase Authentication for user management.

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.3 AnimeFillerList

We fetch filler episode information from animefillerlist.com to provide filler detection features. No personal data is sent to this service.

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your data may only be shared in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We may share information with your explicit consent
  • Service Providers: We share data with Firebase/Google Cloud solely for the purpose of providing the sync service
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

7.1 Right to Access

You can view all your stored data directly in the extension popup at any time.

7.2 Right to Rectification

You can modify your data by editing episode progress within the extension.

7.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can delete all your data:

  • Local Data: Use the "Clear Data" button in the extension settings
  • Cloud Data: When you clear data while signed in, it is also deleted from our servers
  • Complete Deletion: Contact us at thomasthanos28@gmail.com to request complete account deletion

7.4 Right to Data Portability

You can export your data. Contact us for a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

7.5 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can sign out at any time to stop cloud synchronization. Your local data will remain, but no new data will be synced.

8. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored indefinitely until you clear it or uninstall the extension
  • Cloud Data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted upon your request or account deletion
  • Video Progress: Progress for incomplete episodes is retained for 7 days, with a maximum of 20 entries

9. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • For significant changes, providing a notice in the extension

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
